Constanța, Romania

My latest adventure took me to Constanța, Romania’s vibrant coastal city on the shores of the Black Sea. The mix of ancient history and laid-back seaside charm made it unforgettable. I wandered through the Old Town, where cobblestone streets led me to the majestic Casino, a stunning art nouveau building right by the water. A visit to the Roman mosaics museum felt like stepping back in time, and I soaked in the energy of the bustling marina. Of course, no trip would be complete without lounging on Mamaia Beach and indulging in some fresh seafood. Constanța’s blend of culture, history, and relaxation has definitely stolen a piece of my heart!

My Favorite Destinations

  • Barcelona, Spain

    Barcelona is a city that never ceases to amaze. From the intricate designs of Gaudí’s Sagrada Familia and Park Güell to the lively La Rambla, every corner bursts with color and culture. The city's beach, Barceloneta, offers a perfect spot to relax after exploring the historic Gothic Quarter. The food scene is unforgettable too, with tapas, seafood, and endless sangria filling the streetside cafes. Barcelona’s mix of modernist architecture, vibrant neighborhoods, and Mediterranean vibes make it one of my all-time favorite destinations.

    Learn more about Barcelona


  • Bari, Italy

    Bari, on Italy’s Adriatic coast, is the perfect blend of old-world charm and coastal beauty. The narrow, winding streets of Bari Vecchia (the Old Town) are alive with local culture, where I enjoyed delicious orecchiette pasta and fresh seafood. The stunning Basilica di San Nicola is a must-see, and the harbor views are simply magical at sunset. Bari also serves as a gateway to explore the Puglia region, with its nearby olive groves, ancient towns, and crystal-clear beaches.

    Learn more about Bari


  • Sighisoara, Romania

    Sighisoara feels like stepping into a fairytale, with its colorful medieval buildings, cobblestone streets, and fortified walls. This enchanting Transylvanian town is known as the birthplace of Vlad the Impaler, the inspiration for Dracula, adding a layer of mystery to its charm. Climbing the Clock Tower offers stunning views of the town’s red rooftops, and wandering through the narrow alleyways reveals hidden gems like cozy cafes and artisan shops. Sighisoara’s blend of rich history, Gothic allure, and old-world beauty makes it one of Romania’s most magical destinations.

    Learn more about Sighisoara

Destination Dates Activities

Vienna, Austria

 December 2024
Vienna is magical during the holiday season, with it's famous Christmas markets and festive decorations.
  1. Schönbrunn Palace – Opulent Baroque palace with vast gardens.
  2. St. Stephen’s Cathedral – Gothic cathedral with stunning architecture.
  3. Belvedere Palace – Art museum housing works by Gustav Klimt.
  4. Hofburg Palace – Former imperial residence and now home to museums.
  5. Prater Park – Amusement park with the famous giant Ferris wheel.

Lisbon, Portugal

 April 2025
The city experiences mild weather and fewer tourists, making it great for sightseeing and outdoor cafes.
  1. Belém Tower – A UNESCO World Heritage Site and symbol of Portugal’s Age of Discovery.
  2. Jerónimos Monastery – Another UNESCO site with stunning Manueline architecture.
  3. Alfama District – The oldest neighborhood in Lisbon, known for Fado music.
  4. Lisbon Oceanarium – One of the largest aquariums in the world.
  5. Praça do Comércio – A grand plaza overlooking the Tagus River.

Nice, France

 July 2024
The French Riviera sparkles in the summer, and Nice offers a blend of culture and seaside relaxation.
  1. Promenade des Anglais – A famous waterfront promenade perfect for walks along the beach.
  2. Castle Hill (Colline du Château) – Enjoy panoramic views of Nice and the Mediterranean from this hilltop park.
  3. Cours Saleya Market – A vibrant outdoor market selling fresh produce and local flowers.
  4. Musée Matisse – A museum dedicated to the works of French artist Henri Matisse.
  5. Villefranche-sur-Mer – A picturesque nearby town with a beautiful beach and old town to explore.
